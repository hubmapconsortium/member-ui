EPC

External Program Consultants (EPC) are non-members who offer independent oversight and input on implementation strategy for the Consortium. EPC members should register for an account, however, to be given access to Consortium documents and other collateral.

Associate Member

HuBMAP offers researchers not currently funded by HuBMAP the opportunity to apply for Associate Member status. Associate Members are expected to contribute to the research goals of the Consortium by generating, sharing or analyzing data; jointly developing software or algorithms; developing mutually beneficial resources, protocols or reagents; or coordinating development of standards, formats or metadata. You must be approved as an Associate Member before you submit this registration request. More information and details on how to apply.
